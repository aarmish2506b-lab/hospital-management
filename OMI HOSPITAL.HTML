Our Medical Services

We offer a wide range of medical services to meet all your healthcare needs.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care with advanced diagnostic and treatment options for all cardiac conditions.

Neurology

Expert care for disorders of the nervous system, including brain and spinal cord conditions.

Pediatrics

Specialized medical care for infants, children, and adolescents with a family-centered approach.

Orthopedics

Treatment for musculoskeletal injuries and conditions, from sports injuries to joint replacement.

Ophthalmology

Complete eye care services including vision correction, cataract surgery, and treatment for eye diseases.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency services with a dedicated team ready to handle any medical emergency.

About OMI Hospital

Founded in 1995, OMI Hospital has been at the forefront of medical excellence, providing compassionate care and innovative treatments to our community.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology, and our team of dedicated healthcare professionals is committed to delivering the highest standard of patient care.

We believe in a holistic approach to healthcare, focusing not just on treating illnesses but on promoting overall wellness and prevention.
