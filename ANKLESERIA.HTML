About Ankleseria Hospital

Founded in 1985, Ankleseria Hospital has been at the forefront of medical excellence, providing comprehensive healthcare services to our community for over three decades.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional patient care through advanced medical technology, highly skilled professionals, and a compassionate approach to healing.

With over 200 beds, state-of-the-art operation theaters, and advanced diagnostic facilities, we are equipped to handle a wide range of medical conditions.
