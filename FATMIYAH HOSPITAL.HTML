About Fatmiyah Hospital

Established with a vision to provide exceptional healthcare services, Fatmiyah Hospital has been serving the community for over 20 years. Our commitment to patient-centered care and medical excellence has made us a trusted healthcare provider.

We combine advanced medical technology with compassionate care to ensure the best outcomes for our patients. Our team of dedicated healthcare professionals works tirelessly to provide personalized treatment plans.

  • State-of-the-art medical equipment
  • Experienced and certified medical staff
  • 24/7 emergency services
  • Patient-centered approach to care
  • Multiple specialty departments
  • International quality standards
