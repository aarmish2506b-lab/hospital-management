About Medicare Hospital

Learn more about our history, mission, and commitment to healthcare excellence.

Providing Quality Healthcare Since 1985

Medicare General Hospital has been serving the community for over three decades with a commitment to excellence in patient care. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology and staffed by highly skilled healthcare professionals.

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the illness, and our patient-centered approach ensures that you receive personalized care tailored to your specific needs.

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to all members of our community, regardless of their background or financial situation.
